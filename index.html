About Me — The Food Man

With passion for real, good food.

I’m Hannibal Lecter, a sandwich‑obsessed home cook who believes that simple ingredients, treated well, make unforgettable bites. Every week I test new pairings, tweak sauces, and share the keepers here so you can recreate them at home without fancy gear.

My recipes focus on seasonal produce, quality bread, and make‑ahead components to save your time on busy days. You’ll find quick lunches for the office, hearty weekend brunch ideas, and sweet croissant treats for when the craving hits. If you try a recipe, tell me how it went—your tweaks often become the next crowd favorite.
